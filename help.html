How Can We Help You?

Your safety is our priority. Explore solutions or ask our AI assistant for help.

What is ShareRide?

ShareRide is a ride-sharing platform that lets people book or offer rides to save cost, reduce traffic, and travel conveniently.

How do I book a ride?

Go to the Rider page, enter pickup and drop, check available rides, and confirm your booking.

How can drivers share their ride?

Drivers can go to the Driver page, enter pickup, drop, vehicle type, fare, and share their ride.

Can I cancel my ride?

Yes, you can cancel before confirmation or directly from your active ride section.

How do I pay for my ride?

Currently, payment is cash-only, but online payment options will be added soon.
